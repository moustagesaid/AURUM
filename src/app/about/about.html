The Essence of Aurum

A LEGACY IN EVERY DROP

At AURUM, we compose fragrance like fine jewelry—layer by layer, note by note. From the first shimmer of bergamot to the slow warmth of amber, each blend is crafted to linger with quiet confidence.

Our signature accords are anchored in rare woods and precious resins—smoked oud, sunlit saffron, and velvet musk—balanced with modern clarity. The result is a scent that feels timeless, intimate, and unmistakably yours.
